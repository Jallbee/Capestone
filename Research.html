Research

My experience at Pax East as well as working on a social media team, I have become fascinated by how these companies use social media for marketing. For the research part I decided to focus my research on social media marketing and how specifically the gaming industry uses social media. I also Reached out to Arrowhead and Intersloth to hear first hand about how social media effected the growth of their games


Arrowhead

Arrowhead is the game studio who created Helldivers2. Helldivers2 is a live service game meaning that the content is coming out as the game is being played and the actions in the game change the course of what comes next. All of this is being led by one single developer named Joel who is the "Game Master". All of this led to the amazing bost of players nearing 411,000 and crashing the servers. All of this success is all thanks to social media and how fast it was able to spread the ideas behind Helldivers and get fans of the game to get their friends to play. I reached out to the Arrowhead team but unfortunately never received a response.


Intersloth

Intersloth is the game studio who created Among us. Among Us was released in 2018 and was actually almost considered a flop as it did not get nearly as many players as they expected. Until the pandemic hit and streamers were looking for a new and different game to play with friends from home. This is one social media took Among us and has become the amazing phenomenon it is now. I reached out to the Intersloth team as well but as they are busy making a movie with Among Us I unfortunately never received a response.


Social media has revolutionized marketing in general, but if there is one industry who is using social media the most and that would be the gaming industry. Social media has been crucial for the gaming industry for a few key reasons. The first is that it allows game developers and companies to directly connect with players, share updates and get feedback. Next it makes it huge for building a community around the game. With this connection it boosts player engagement.

With the interactive nature of social media it also enables better communication with the developers sometimes even before the game is released. Developers have been able to collaborate with players by using social media to release beta testing and even crowdsourcing ideas. With not only the popularity of social media but also the gaming community many companies have also used social media and video games to hold contests and even marketing in the games themselves.
